The Midnight Library by Matt Haig is a novel that follows the life of Nora Seed, who finds herself in a library between
life and death, with the opportunity to try out alternate versions of her life and
find the one where she truly belongs.

THE AGANTHA CAVES
Sculpture

The Agantha Caves, located in Maharashtra, India, are ancient rock-cut Buddhist monuments
dating from the 2nd century BCE to the 6th century CE.
Known for their intricate sculptures, vibrant paintings, and depictions of Buddhist and Hindu themes, these caves are a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a significant
cultural and historical landmark in India.
